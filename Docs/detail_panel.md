### Details Panel

**Press and hold the cover image to open a details panel.**  

**This panel provides**:  
- Multiple titles  
  - English title  
  - Original title  
  - Romaji title  
  - And more  
- Format information  
- Release status (e.g., Releasing, Completed, etc.)  
- Synopsis  
- Statistics  
- Ratings from multiple sources  
- External links (to different sites and sources)
